Our Story

Dusk & Dawn Safari was founded on a belief that Tanzania’s wilderness deserves a guiding company that reflects its beauty, integrity, and depth. What began as a small Arusha-based operation has grown into a fully integrated safari service built on reliability, hospitality, and a deep respect for the land.

By managing our fleet and operations directly, we ensure every journey is safe, seamless, and unforgettable. Our mission is to deliver a safari experience that honors both the landscapes we explore and the guests who trust us with their adventure.

Why We Chose the Name “Dusk & Dawn”

Dusk and dawn are the two sacred thresholds of the African wilderness—moments when the savannah shifts, predators stir, and the world reveals its quiet truth. These hours symbolize transition, clarity, and renewal. They are the bookends of every great journey.

We chose Dusk & Dawn because these moments mirror the heart of our work: guiding guests with intention, integrity, and reverence for the land.
