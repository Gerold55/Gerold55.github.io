Our Story

Dusk & Dawn Safari was born from the shared heart of an interracial family who found common ground in the wild places of Tanzania. With roots in two cultures and a mutual love for wildlife, we built this company to share the beauty, peace, and wonder of the African bush with others. For us, the wilderness is more than a destination — it’s where our worlds came together. Every sunrise and sunset reminds us of the balance between our backgrounds, our journeys, and the life we’re building as a family.

Why We Chose the Dik-Dik as our mascot!

Our mascot, the dik-dik, carries that same spirit. Small, alert, and full of character, it moves through the savannah with the same rhythm as the sun — rising and setting, playing tag with the moon. Its presence reflects the heart of our name, Dusk & Dawn, and the harmony we hope every guest feels when they travel with us. We created this company to open the door for others to experience the wild the way we do — with respect, curiosity, and a sense of belonging.
